Guided services

Experiences & Services We Offer

Book a fixed departure, add a guided day tour, or ask us to build a private programme around your target species, group size, and comfort level.

Guided Day Tours

Birding, safaris, trails, and wetlands

Half-day and full-day guiding for guests already in a region, with routes matched to the season, local wildlife, and your pace.

Best for: Travellers, resort guests, families

Private & Tailor-Made Tours

Your dates, your region, your comfort level

A bespoke India itinerary built around your interests, fitness, wish-list species, preferred accommodation, and available travel window.

Best for: Couples, friends, solo travellers

Target Species Routes

Wildlife with a clear purpose

Plan routes for tigers, leopards, elephants, hornbills, pheasants, red pandas, snow leopards, frogs, butterflies, and other India wildlife highlights.

Best for: Birders, naturalists, photographers

Wildlife Photography Walks

Big cats, birds, macro, and fieldcraft

Patient field support for ethical photography of mammals, birds, frogs, butterflies, forest details, landscapes, and safari subjects.

Best for: Photographers of all levels

Night Walks & Spotlighting

The wild after dark

Guided night sessions for frogs, geckos, owls, moths, civets, insects, and forest soundscapes, run only where conditions and rules allow.

Best for: Add-on for suitable routes

Culture & Landscape Trails

People, places, and wild country

Add villages, tea, coffee, spice, desert, river, craft, coastal, or mountain experiences between wildlife sessions.

Best for: Families and slow travellers

Custom Group Programmes

Made for your group

Flexible programmes for private groups, clubs, companies, families, schools, universities, and international travel groups.

Best for: Groups of 6 or more

School & University Options

Academic when you need it

Field courses, student camps, and curriculum-linked modules remain available on request, but they are built as custom programmes.

Best for: Schools and universities
