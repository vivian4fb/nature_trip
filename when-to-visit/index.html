When to Visit India for Wildlife

India is not one season or one habitat. Tiger forests, wetlands, deserts, mountains, rainforests, and coasts each peak at different times.

Safaris

Big Cats & Forests

October - June

Central and western forest safaris run strongest outside the heavy monsoon, with dry months often best for big-cat movement.

Birds

Birding & Wetlands

October - March

Winter brings migrants, comfortable weather, wetland spectacle, desert birds, raptors, cranes, and strong all-round birding.

Rainforest

Frogs & Monsoon Life

June - September

Rainforest routes peak during the monsoon for frogs, reptiles, insects, waterfalls, and lush forest atmosphere.

Mountains

Himalayan Wildlife

March - May / Oct - Nov

Spring and autumn are usually best for Himalayan birds, forest walks, mountain views, and comfortable travel.
